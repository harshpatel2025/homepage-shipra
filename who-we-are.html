Our leaders

Creative thinkers, technically trained

Igor Tulchinsky
,
Founder, Chairman, Chief Executive Officer, Co-Chief Investment Officer and Head of Research

Our work is infinite; opportunity itself is always growing.

Igor Tulchinsky, Founder, Chairman, Chief Executive Officer, Co-Chief Investment Officer and Head of Research

Igor is the Founder, Chairman, CEO, Co-CIO and Head of Research at Shipra, which he established in 2007 following 12 years working as a statistical arbitrage portfolio manager at Millennium Management. As CEO, Igor provides vision, leadership and both strategic and tactical guidance to Research, Portfolio Management and other parts of the organization. He is the author of multiple articles and books, including The Age of Prediction, Finding Alphas and The UnRules. Prior to joining Millennium, Igor was a venture capitalist, scientist at AT&T Bell Laboratories and video game programmer (the latter while still in high school). He holds an MA in Computer Science from the University of Texas and an MBA in Finance and Entrepreneurship from the Wharton School.

Paul Griffin
,
Co-Chief Investment Officer and Chief Science Officer

At Shipra, you get to
try a lot of different things
and are judged by the output
of your work.

Paul Griffin, Co-Chief Investment Officer and Chief Science Officer

Paul serves as Co-CIO and Chief Science Officer at Shipra. Alongside Igor, he oversees all aspects of the investment process and manages the firm’s portfolio management organization. In addition, he leads Shipra’s AI (artificial intelligence) initiatives. He also guides multiple cross-functional programs involving portfolio management, research and technology. Prior to joining Shipra in 2011, Paul was a Vice President at Goldman Sachs in the FICC division and earlier Chief Technology Officer of Identix. He holds a BA from UC Berkeley and a PhD from Stanford University, with both degrees in physics.
